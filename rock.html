Vietnamese Rock Music

Vietnamese rock music has evolved significantly since the late 1990s, developing a passionate following and unique identity that blends Western rock influences with Vietnamese cultural elements.

While not as commercially dominant as Vpop, Vietnamese rock has carved out an important niche in the country's music scene with dedicated fans and influential bands that have pushed musical boundaries and addressed social themes through their lyrics.

From hard rock to alternative and indie rock, Vietnamese rock artists continue to develop the genre and build bridges with the international rock community through festivals and collaborations.

Notable Vietnamese Rock Bands

Bức Tường (The Wall)

Pioneers of Vietnamese rock music, Bức Tường was formed in 1995 and is considered the most influential rock band in Vietnam's history.

Microwave

Known for their alternative rock sound and thoughtful lyrics, Microwave has built a dedicated following in Vietnam's rock scene.

Ngủ Cùng Sao (Sleep With Stars)

A band known for their melodic rock style and poetic lyrics that has captured the hearts of Vietnamese rock enthusiasts.

Rock Music Festivals in Vietnam

Vietnam has developed a growing rock festival scene that provides platforms for both established and emerging rock artists to showcase their talent.

RockStorm

One of Vietnam's largest rock music festivals, RockStorm tours major cities across the country, featuring the best of Vietnamese rock music alongside international acts.

Vietnam Rock Meeting

An annual gathering of rock enthusiasts that focuses on supporting underground and independent rock bands, providing them with exposure to wider audiences.
