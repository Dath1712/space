Vietnamese Pop Music (Vpop)

Vpop is a genre of Vietnamese popular music that emerged in the early 2000s and has since gained immense popularity both within Vietnam and internationally. It combines elements of Western pop music with traditional Vietnamese musical styles and themes.

Modern Vpop artists blend contemporary music production techniques with Vietnamese vocal styles and lyrics. Many Vpop songs address themes of love, youth, and social issues relevant to Vietnamese society.

The genre has seen significant growth in recent years, with many Vpop artists achieving international recognition and collaborating with musicians from other Asian countries.

Popular Vpop Artists

Son Tung MTP

One of the most influential Vpop artists who has revolutionized the industry with his unique style and international collaborations.

My Tam

Known as the "Queen of Vpop," My Tam has been a dominant force in Vietnamese music for over two decades.
