Charity-Music for hope

Charity Music Festival 2024: Green Returns

Organized on September 29, 2024, at Eco Central Park in Nghe An by the Nghe An Radio and Television Station.

This program is part of the "Sao Mai Nghe 2024" series, aiming to support people affected by storms and floods.

All donated funds will go to families who lost loved ones during the floods in Nghe An.

We Love Vietnam Showcase

Held on September 22, 2024, in Ho Chi Minh City with the participation of many famous artists such as Van Mai Huong, Ly Ly, tlinh, Han Sara, My Anh, Hoang Duyen, Linh Ka, and others.

All ticket proceeds are donated to the Vietnam Fatherland Front to help northern provinces recover from the effects of Storm No. 3.

Fundraising Concert "Vo"

Held on January 20, 2024, in Hanoi, in collaboration with the Warm Scarves for Children Fund and companies such as Sconnect Academy and Cozy. This event aims to raise funds to support the Embroidery Club at De Xu Phinh Boarding School. Established in 2007, the club is a gathering place for music lovers who want to use music to make life more beautiful.

Warm Winter Charity Concerts 2024

An annual event aimed at raising funds to support children with disabilities, through performances by famous singers and promising young artists in Vietnamese music.

VNSO Christmas Charity Concert 2023

This charity concert was held at 7:30 PM on December 26, 2023, at St. Joseph's Cathedral in Hanoi. Conductor Honna Tetsuji led the performance, featuring chamber music artists such as Bui Son Tung (oboe), Pham Van Hieu (trumpet), and many other talented musicians. The program included famous works by composers like Henri Charpentier, Alessandro Marcello, and Antonio Vivaldi.

The 7th "Autumn Love" Concert

This annual classical music concert took place on October 21, 2023, at the Vietnam National Academy of Music, aiming to raise funds for underprivileged children undergoing treatment at K Hospital in Hanoi. It received support from the Vietnam National Academy of Music, the Piano Department of the Academy, the Compassion Fund, the Warm Scarves for Children Fund, and the May Space Music Academy.
